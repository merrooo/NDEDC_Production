🔷 1. Positive Sequence Impedance Z₁ (Ω/km)

—

📖 Discussion

Positive sequence impedance \(Z_1 = R_{ac} + j X_1\).
\(X_1 = 0.06283 \ln(D_{eq}/GMR)\) Ω/km at 50/60 Hz. Larger spacing increases reactance; larger conductor radius reduces it.

\( Z_1 = R_{ac} + j \cdot 0.06283 \ln(D_{eq}/GMR) \) Ω/km

🌍 2. Zero Sequence Impedance Z₀ (Ω/km)

📐 Z₀ Calculator

—

📖 Discussion

Zero sequence includes earth return: \(R_e = 9.88\times10^{-4}f\) Ω/km, \(D_e = 658.5\sqrt{\rho/f}\) m. Shield wires increase reactance via factor k_sw.

\( Z_0 = (R_{ac}+3R_e) + j k_{sw} \cdot 0.06283 \ln(D_e / \sqrt[3]{GMR \cdot D^2}) \)

🔁 3. Zero-Sequence Compensation Factor k₀

—

📖 Discussion

\(k_0 = (Z_0 - Z_1)/(3Z_1)\) compensates ground faults. \(Z_{AG} = V_A/(I_A + k_0 I_{res})\).

\( k_0 = \dfrac{Z_0 - Z_1}{3 Z_1} \) (complex)
